PSYC&100- General Psychology

Professor Leo Kiralla

I took this class in Fall Quarter 2021. Previously, I had no knowledge of Psychology besides the concepts used in TV shows like 'Criminal Minds'. Of course, many of the psychology popular in media is false or misleading. With this class, you can learn a lot about yourself and the people around you. You learn the basic terms and concepts of psychology, and can build upon that knowledge in future classes. After taking the class, I had a new perspective on mental health and mental processes. Teach your brain more about itself!
